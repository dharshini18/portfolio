The project encompasses a novel steganographic approach for the secure transmission of keys (information or a secret message) between communicators.

If you want to read more about the project, kindly refer to the paper published in International Journal of Applied Engineering Research - ISSN 0973-4562 Volume 9, Number 7 (2014) pp. 763-769.
